Problem Statement

In today’s digital age, college students often struggle to manage multiple academic deadlines, extracurricular commitments, and personal goals efficiently. Many rely on paper planners or scattered apps that lack integration and real-time reminders. This often leads to missed deadlines, reduced productivity, and increased stress levels.

The goal of this project is to design a centralized and intuitive web-based task management tool specifically for students. The platform will allow users to track assignments, set automated reminders, and visualize workload distribution using color-coded calendars and progress indicators. By addressing this issue, students can better manage time, maintain balance, and improve academic performance.
